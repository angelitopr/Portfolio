The Basic logic of Blockchain

Blockchain is considered a system where it takes information that is very difficult to change or hack. Blockchain is a digital record of transactions that copied over and over then distribued all over the network of computers in the blockchain itself. A block in the chain contains a number of the transactions made as well as a record of the transaction adds on every participant record. The decentralised database that is managed by many participants rather known as the DLT. Blockchain is just a type of DLT where the transactions that are recorded then in a cryptographic signature essentially that is called a hash. The benefits of such techology is that it is proogrammable and malleable with what are called smart contracts, all the records in it are encypted, the id of all the ones that paarticipate in such technology is anonymous, all the network participants are unanimously agreed, it is completely transparent, the records are set and connot be changed. Blockchain is completely secure because lets ssay a hacker wanted to change the chain it would have to change every single block in the chain which is technnically impossible. Examples of this technology are Bitcoin and ethereum these are only one of the many blockchain sysytems.
